for_BBS: data folder: 
includes data that we need for the analysis, for details see below:
Pardieck, K.L., Ziolkowski Jr., D.J., Lutmerding, M., Aponte, V.I., and Hudson, M-A.R., 2020, 
North American Breeding Bird Survey Dataset 1966 - 2019: U.S. Geological Survey data release, 
https://doi.org/10.5066/P9J6QUF6.

Details for strata is also provided as BBS_physiographic_strataname.csv (source: https://www.pwrc.usgs.gov/bbs/StrataNames/index.html)
