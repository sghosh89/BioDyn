data folder for extended version of BioTIMEx 
raw_data: data downloaded when it is not included in BioTIME database
wrangled_data: 
	Some basic selection criteria were followed:
	- atleast 20 years of abundance/biomass/count data.
	- nested sites within a single STUDY_ID  were considered.
	- consistent sampling effort (monthly frequency, common months, controlled plots) throughout the study period.
	- >=2 species were found for atleast 70% of study period.
